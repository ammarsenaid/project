# FachkundePilot — Project Blueprint

## 1) Product Vision
FachkundePilot is a multi-certificate German learning SaaS for regulated professional exams. It provides one consistent learning engine across many certificates, combining structured lessons, exam-style practice, vocabulary training, flashcards, and progress tracking.

### Vision Statement
> **"One platform to prepare confidently for German professional certifications with clear structure, multilingual explanations, and exam-focused practice."**

## 2) Target Users

### Primary Segments
1. **Career starters and career changers in Germany** preparing for mandatory industry certifications.
2. **Migrants and non-native German speakers** who need additional language support while learning German exam content.
3. **Working professionals** who need flexible, mobile-first, self-paced exam preparation.

### Secondary Segments
- Training centers that want supplementary digital practice tools.
- Employers onboarding staff for regulated roles.

## 3) Problem Statement
Current preparation options are fragmented, often certificate-specific, and frequently outdated in UX quality. Learners struggle with:
- Inconsistent course structures across different certificates.
- Limited multilingual support while exams remain German.
- Weak repetition systems and poor progress visibility.
- Low-trust, low-quality interfaces that reduce motivation.

## 4) Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
- **Multi-certificate from day one** (not single-exam locked).
- **German-first, multilingual support** (DE + AR + EN + TR explanations).
- **Reusable learning engine** for lessons, flashcards, mock exams, glossary, and progress.
- **Premium and trustworthy UX** designed for serious exam success.

## 5) Supported Certificates (Initial Catalog Direction)
- Taxi und Mietwagen Fachkundeprüfung
- §34a Bewachungsgewerbe
- Güterkraftverkehr Fachkunde
- Omnibusverkehr Fachkunde
- Versicherung §34d
- Finanzanlagen §34f
- Immobiliardarlehen §34i
- Freiverkäufliche Arzneimittel
- Gefahrgut / ADR
- Gastgewerbe-Unterrichtung
- Extensible to additional certificates later

## 6) Multilingual Learning Logic
1. Present **original German term/question** first.
2. Offer **simple explanation translation** in AR/EN/TR.
3. Reinforce key German vocabulary with glossary and flashcards.
4. Practice via mock exams in German style and wording.
5. Track progress and readiness per certificate and section.

### Content Principle
- **German source of truth** for exam authenticity.
- Localized explanation layers are assistive, not replacements.

## 7) Business Model Ideas
- **Freemium**: limited lessons + sample exams.
- **Subscription tiers**:
  - Basic: 1 certificate access.
  - Pro: multi-certificate + full mock exams.
  - Premium: advanced analytics + tutor features (future).
- **B2B / institutional licensing** for academies and employers.
- Optional add-ons: certificate bundles, intensive prep tracks.

## 8) MVP Scope
### In Scope (MVP)
- Public pages (home, certificates, pricing, auth entry points).
- User dashboard and enrollment in certificates.
- Core learning flow: sections, lessons, flashcards, mock exam basics.
- Progress tracking essentials.
- Admin content CRUD for certificates and learning assets.
- Multilingual explanation rendering (DE/AR/EN/TR fields).

### Out of Scope (MVP)
- AI tutoring copilot.
- Full-scale adaptive learning algorithm.
- Complex enterprise org management.
- Deep BI analytics suite.

## 9) Future Expansion Scope
- AI-powered explanation simplification and tutoring.
- Personalized study plans and adaptive difficulty.
- Spaced repetition optimization engine.
- Certificate-specific math simulators and calculators.
- Instructor dashboards and cohort tracking.
- Native mobile apps (iOS/Android).

## 10) Product Principles
1. **Exam realism** over gamification gimmicks.
2. **Consistency** across all certificates.
3. **Clarity** for multilingual learners.
4. **Trust** through design and transparent progress.
5. **Scalability** in content model and architecture.
